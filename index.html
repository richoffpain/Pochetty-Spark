FAQs
What does unlimited really mean ?

With all our plans, you can submit as many requests as you like and we will work on them every business day. Revisions included. No matter how many requests or revisions you submit, the price stays the same. You are only limited in terms of size of each individual task and our speed.

What types of taks can I request ?

The answer is a lot! As long as it’s work related to your Shopify store. If you can clearly explain or show it, then we can design and develop it. If you feel like you don’t know exactly what you need, our Growth plan includes an audit and improvement suggestions to keep your store constantly improving.

How do I cancel my acccount ?

To cancel your account, simply login here, click My Account in the top navigation and proceed to cancel your subscription.

Can my clients use the services ?

Yes, you can use our team to build your client stores without them knowing about it. However, we will also never communicate with your clients under your brand name.
